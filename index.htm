System Purpose and Benefits

The Chest X-ray Anomalies Detection System is a web-based tool designed to assist in the analysis and diagnosis of lung diseases through the interpretation of chest X-rays. Utilizing deep learning algorithms, the system predicts the presence of 14 anomalies, aiding healthcare professionals in making accurate diagnoses.

Benefits of the system include:

  • Enhanced chest X-ray diagnosis.
  • Easy accessibility via internet connection, making it available to a wide range of users.
  • Second-hand opinions on chest X-rays, providing reassurance and validation to primary diagnostics.
  • Opportunity for training and exposure to deep learning technologies.
